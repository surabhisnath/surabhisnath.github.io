I graduated from IIIT Delhi in July 2020 with a B. Tech. (Hons) in Computer Science & Engineering and a minor area in Computational Biology. I will now be joining the Max Planck School of Cognition, Germany for my doctoral studies in September 2020. For my Masters, I will be enrolling into the Berlin School of Mind and Brain. I am very enthusiastic to use statistical modelling, deep learning and reinforcement learning to understand human cognitive phenomena such as affect, decision making, improvisation and music perception.

I am currently working on a project with Prof. Vinoo Alluri, IIIT Hyderabad where we aim to make correlations between audio representations capturing musical properties, with fMRI data collected during naturalistic music listening. I am also working on an RL framework to model decision making in individuals with depression, with Prof. Haomiao Jin, USC.

Apart from my research interests, I have learnt Hindustani Classical music for over 10 years and love singing. I also hold National scholarship for Kuchipudi dance. In my school days, I used to actively participate in athletics! Recently, I have enjoyed trying out some voice acting.
